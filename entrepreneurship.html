Nandan Nilekani

An Indian entrepreneur. He became one of the founders of Infosys and is the non-executive Chairman of Infosys at Ekesh hotels, succeeding R. Seshasai and Ravi Venkatesan, who were co-chairmen of the Board, on August 24, 2017.  After the departure of Veshal Sikki, Nilekani was appointed non-executive Chairman of the Board on August 24, 2017. He was the Chairman of the Office of Unique Identification of India. After a successful career at Infosys, he headed the Technology Committee of the Government of India, TAGUP. He is a member of the Indian National Congress, but has not been actively involved in politics since 2019.
